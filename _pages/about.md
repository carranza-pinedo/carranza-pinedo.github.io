I am a philosopher of language whose research lies at the intersection of formal pragmatics and social cognition. I study how communication carries evaluative and expressive meaning, especially in the case of slurs, pejoratives, and related terms. I also work on affect and social signalling in animal communication and early vocal development.

I am Adjunct Lecturer at the Universidad Nacional Mayor de San Marcos (Peru). Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Münster (Department of Philosophy) under the supervision of [Ulrich Krohs](https://www.uni-muenster.de/PhilSem/mitglieder/krohs/krohs.html).

My Ph.D. is from the Università degli Studi di Milano and the École Normale Supérieure (Institut Jean Nicod), where I was co-advised by [Elisa Paganini](https://www.unimi.it/en/ugov/person/elisa-paganini) and [Márta Abrusán](https://sites.google.com/site/martaabrusan/).
