From April 2026, I will teach in the Philosophy MA at Universidad Nacional Mayor de San Marcos. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Münster (Department of Philosophy) in the project [“The role of individual internal states in niche choice, niche construction, and behavioural niche conformance”](https://www.uni-bielefeld.de/fakultaeten/biologie/forschung/verbuende/sfb_nc3/projects/d01ph2) (PI: [Ulrich Krohs](https://www.uni-muenster.de/PhilSem/mitglieder/krohs/krohs.html)).

My research lies at the intersection of philosophy of language and the philosophy of emotions, with a focus on affective meaning (slurs, pejoratives, nicknames) and the science of affect (animal emotions, sentience, welfare). 

My Ph.D. is from the Università degli Studi di Milano and the École Normale Supérieure (Institut Jean Nicod), where I was co-advised by [Elisa Paganini](https://www.unimi.it/en/ugov/person/elisa-paganini) and [Márta Abrusán](https://sites.google.com/site/martaabrusan/).
